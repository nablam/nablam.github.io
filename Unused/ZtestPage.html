Pi Web Rover

I made this monstrosity a while back. I was inspired by the curiosity rover and wanted to have a spy bot that I can control from fork to keep an eye on our cat's activities .

Using a raspberry pI B+ , I setup Lighttp server and created a simple html page with controll buttons and a video frame. I then Linked the buttomn acctions with bash scripts to controll the 4 motors.
